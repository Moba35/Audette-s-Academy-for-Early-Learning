ABOUT US

  • It is the philosophy of Audette's Academy for Early Learning that children should experience happiness and success. Children are our future and should be nurtured in an atmosphere that is comfortable, safe, and secure. Children learn through many activities play and interacting with others.

  • The purpose and mission of the center is to promote a social, and emotional development of young children through planned activities and group interactions by creating a loving and learning environment in which children will be prepared for a smooth entry into the school system.

  • We do not discriminate against anyone regardless of race, creed, religion, nationality, or children with special needs. Your children will only be released to you or the people whose names are on your emergency information card. Under no circumstances will anyone else be allowed to pick up your child unless we have direct permission from you.
